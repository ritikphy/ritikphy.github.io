Ritik Dubey

I am a BS–MS Physics student at IISER Kolkata with a strong interest in quantum information theory, quantum foundations, resource theory, and quantum measurement.

My current work is centered on theoretical and computational ideas in quantum physics. I am especially interested in questions where mathematical structure, information, and measurement meet.

This site is intentionally minimalist so that professors and collaborators can quickly find what matters: my current work, my projects, my notes, and a simple way to contact me.
